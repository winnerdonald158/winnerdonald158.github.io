Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by telecom companies. Losing existing customers directly impacts revenue and increases acquisition costs. This project analyzes customer data to identify churn patterns and uncover the factors that influence customer retention.

Goal

Identify the key drivers of customer churn and provide insights that can help telecom companies improve customer retention.

Process

  • Cleaned and transformed customer data.
  • Performed exploratory analysis using SQL and Excel.
  • Built KPIs to track churn performance.
  • Created interactive Power BI dashboards.
  • Analyzed customer segments and churn behavior.
  • Identified customers with higher risk of leaving.

Key Insights

  • Customers with shorter tenure showed significantly higher churn rates.
  • Monthly contract customers were more likely to leave than long-term contract customers.
  • Certain service combinations were associated with increased churn risk.
  • High-value customers represented an important retention opportunity.
