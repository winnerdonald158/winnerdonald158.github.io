Project Overview

This project is use to understand how healthcare coverage impacts preventable cancer deaths globally.

Goal

To explore global cancer trends and identify healthcare coverage gaps, risk factors, and preventable deaths.

Process

  • I used PostgreSQL to analyze cancer cases, deaths, healthcare coverage, and prevention data across countries.
  • I compared outcomes across income groups to understand disparities in healthcare access.
  • I evaluated the relationship between healthcare coverage and preventable deaths.
  • I built a Power BI dashboard to visualize global cancer trends and healthcare impact.

Key Insights

  • I found that low-income countries experienced higher levels of preventable cancer deaths.
  • I observed that higher healthcare coverage was associated with better survival outcomes.
  • I identified countries with high death rates and low healthcare coverage as priority areas for intervention.
  • I highlighted regions where improvements in healthcare access could save more lives.
