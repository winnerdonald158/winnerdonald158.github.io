Project Overview

Fraudulent transactions can lead to significant financial losses, damage customer trust, and expose businesses to operational risks. This project analyzes financial transaction data to uncover fraud patterns, identify suspicious customer behavior, and understand where fraud risks are concentrated across transaction categories and merchants.

Goal

To identify high-risk transactions, detect suspicious customer behavior, understand fraud distribution patterns, and estimate the financial impact of fraud.

Process

  • I prepared and structured transaction data for analysis.
  • I used PostgreSQL to answer business questions related to fraud behavior and risk.
  • I analyzed fraud concentration across merchants, customers, and transaction categories.
  • I built an interactive Power BI dashboard to visualize fraud trends and financial impact.

Key Insights

  • I identified merchants that appeared repeatedly in fraudulent transactions.
  • I found that many fraud attempts involved small transactions, suggesting fraudsters were testing stolen payment methods
  • I noticed certain customers appeared multiple times in suspicious activity patterns.
  • Fraud was spread across several transaction categories rather than being limited to one type of transaction.
  • My analysis showed that fraudulent transactions resulted in measurable revenue losses and increased operational risk for the business.
